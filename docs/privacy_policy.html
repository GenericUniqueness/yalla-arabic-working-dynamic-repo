Yalla English Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 8, 2026

Support contact: yallaenglish.app@gmail.com

Overview

Yalla English is an authenticated English-learning app. It supports email/password accounts and Google sign-in.

Information We Collect

Firebase Authentication processes account credentials and status. Email/password sign-in uses the supplied email address. Google sign-in may provide an email address, display name, profile image, provider identifier, and Firebase user identifier.

The current Firestore schema stores account-linked lesson progress, app-use and listening minutes, course totals, streaks, daily goal, favorite lessons, saved words, compact daily usage totals, onboarding completion, and profile first name under the signed-in user's Firebase UID.

Vocabulary quiz batches and history, grammar practice data, settings, progress, favorites, saved words, onboarding state, lesson text, and downloaded audio may also be stored locally on the device.

Diagnostics and Content Delivery

Yalla English uses Firebase Analytics for aggregate app and feature usage. Firebase Analytics may automatically collect app usage events, such as app opens and sessions, along with technical information about the app and device. Firebase and Google Analytics may use pseudonymous app-instance or device identifiers to measure usage and retention.

The app's custom Analytics events include rounded app/listening time, lesson and practice activity counts, question counts, score ranges, and listening milestones. These custom events do not include names, email addresses, Firebase UIDs, saved-word text, clicked-word text, quiz question content, grammar prompts, sentence text, transcripts, or audio.

The app does not include an advertising SDK or dedicated crash-reporting SDK. The app and its providers may still generate limited operational, security, or error logs. Developer authentication diagnostics are limited to debug builds.

Cloudflare R2 or its content delivery network serves lesson audio and content and may process standard network information such as IP address, request time, requested file, and device or browser headers.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to authenticate accounts, synchronize learning progress, provide favorites and review features, deliver lesson content, support offline access, maintain app settings, troubleshoot errors, and protect the service. Yalla English does not sell personal information.

Service Providers

  • Google Firebase Authentication
  • Google Cloud Firestore
  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google Sign-In
  • Cloudflare R2/content delivery

Data Retention

Account-linked data is retained while an account is active or as needed to provide and secure the service. Local preferences and cached content remain until cleared, account deletion is completed, or the app is uninstalled.

Account and Data Deletion

Authenticated users can open Settings > Account > Delete Account. After explicit confirmation and recent authentication, the app deletes documents under users/{currentUid}/data/*, the current Firebase Authentication user, local app preferences and account-linked review data, and cached/downloaded lesson audio and text on that device.

The client is restricted to the current authenticated UID by app logic and Firestore security rules. The app does not use Firebase Admin credentials.

Security

Reasonable technical safeguards are used, including authenticated access and UID-scoped Firestore rules. No internet transmission or storage system is completely secure.

Changes and Contact

This policy may be updated when app behavior, providers, or legal requirements change. Privacy questions or deletion support: yallaenglish.app@gmail.com
